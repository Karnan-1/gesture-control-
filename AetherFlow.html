Stabilization50%
StreamLine — buffers recent positions into a weighted average. High = silky calligraphic strokes. Low = raw, immediate response. Feel the difference in the live pad below.
Flow80%
Paint deposited per pass. Low flow (≈10%) builds colour gradually — perfect for shading & blending. High flow (100%) gives immediate, opaque strokes.

Gestures
How to draw in thin air
🤏
Draw
Pinch thumb and index together to draw. Release pinch to stop instantly.
✋
Erase
Open palm — all four fingers extended and spread wide. Sweep over areas to erase. Erase radius scales with brush size.
🤏✌️
Grab & Move
Keep index + middle open and pinch with thumb to drag the whole drawing layer.
☝️
Hover
Index finger up without pinch keeps cursor active without drawing.
✊
Idle
Close your fist to fully pause. No cursor, no accidental marks. Reposition your hand freely.
Tips for best results
— Keep your hand 30–60 cm from the camera
— Good, even lighting dramatically improves tracking
— Raise Stabilization for lettering & fine art
— Use low Flow (10–30%) for shading & blending
— Tune Debounce if gestures feel unstable
— Keyboard: Ctrl+Z undo · Del clear canvas
